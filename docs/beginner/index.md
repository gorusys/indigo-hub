# Beginner Track

This track is for **newcomers** to Cardano and DeFi. No coding required. By the end you will understand synthetic assets, how to use the Indigo app, and how to mint and use iUSD safely.

## Modules

1. **[Introduction to Cardano & DeFi](intro.md)** — Blockchain and DeFi basics; why synthetic assets exist.
2. **[What is a Synthetic Asset?](synthetic-assets.md)** — iAssets (e.g. iUSD) and how they mirror real-world assets.
3. **[Getting Started with Indigo](using-indigo.md)** — Connect your wallet and use the Indigo web app.
4. **[Collateral and CDPs](collateral-cdps.md)** — How collateralization works and what liquidation means.
5. **[Using Stability Pools](stability-pools.md)** — What Stability Pools are and how to join one.
6. **[Staking INDY & Governance](staking-governance.md)** — INDY token, staking, and community voting.
7. **[Risks and Best Practices](risks-best-practices.md)** — Slippage, coverage, and “not financial advice” disclaimers.

Work through them in order for the best experience.
